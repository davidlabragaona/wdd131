Site Name: Cloudquarium

This name is a combination of Cloud and Aquarium. The idea is to represent the fusion between aquariums and the Cloud.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site can be summarized in 3 aspects

  • Smart tanks: Monitor your fish tank health. Use AI to improve metrics. Provide tutorials to start with the hobby
  • Shoal of fish and users: A platform to foster collaboration among users and enhance the sense of community.
  • B2C and Advertising: Allow users and businesses to connect and offer micro-transactions.

Scenarios

  • How do I start my own fish tank?
  • Can I share my fish tank with others? Comments, rates, tips
  • Can I buy/sell products?
